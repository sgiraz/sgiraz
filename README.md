### `> cat interests.txt`

I've worked in **robotics** — the humanoid kind, where you make machines walk and not fall on their face. That field shaped how I think about software, and it's still where my deepest curiosity lives.

Currently I work in **automotive embedded**, building and testing safety-critical software. It's a field where precision matters and mistakes aren't an option — which is a great school for any engineer. Still, my long-term north star points toward robotics and intelligent machines.

Outside of work, I'm a **maker at heart**: home automation, IoT tinkering, 3D printing, or whatever project lets me blend tech with the analog world. I also love cooking traditional Italian food — the kind that takes time, patience, and zero shortcuts.
